My Trips/Recipes

All the recipes here are inspired by my trips, from the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene landscapes of Athens.

  • Trip to Athens

    During my time in Athens, the discovery of a spectacular Chocolate-filled bread was the culinary impetus for my lava cake, a desert whose intensity echoes the city's rich culture and whose tender texture reflects its evocative atmosphere, and I'm happy to share the full recipe with you here.

  • Roaming around in Rome

    My adventures in Rome led me to discover the art of making authentic Italian pasta from scratch. The vibrant food markets and the aroma of fresh ingredients inspired me to try making Carbonara at home, and I'm excited to share the recipe with you here.

  • Feeling the Delhi-ght

    Exploring the vibrant streets of Delhi was a feast for the senses. The rich aromas and bold flavors I encountered inspired me to create my own version of Chicken Tikka Masala, a dish that captures the essence of Indian cuisine. I'm excited to share this recipe with you here.
