Chicken Tikka Masala

home

A popular Indian dish featuring marinated chicken pieces cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. Best served with naan or rice.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 200g yogurt
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 100ml cream
  • 2 tbsp tikka masala paste
  • Fresh coriander, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and tikka masala paste for at least 1 hour.
  2. In a pan, cook the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree and cream, and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and garnish with fresh coriander.
