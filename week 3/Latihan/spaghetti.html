Spaghetti Carbonara

Home

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, comforting, and perfect for a quick dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and return it to the pot. Quickly toss with the pancetta and its rendered fat.
  5. Remove from heat and immediately pour in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water if needed to achieve desired consistency.
  6. Season with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.
