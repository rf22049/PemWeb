Chocolate Lava Cake

home | back

A decadent dessert with a gooey chocolate center that flows out when you cut into it. Perfect for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients:

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder for greasing the molds

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Melt the dark chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the sugar and eggs until pale and fluffy.
  4. Fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  5. Sift in the flour and gently fold until combined.
  6. Grease the molds with butter and dust with cocoa powder.
  7. Pour the batter into the molds and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  8. Let cool for a minute, then invert onto plates and serve immediately.
